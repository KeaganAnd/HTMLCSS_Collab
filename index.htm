Welcome to Maxwell Scientific

For over 30 years, Maxwell Scientific has been the trusted partner for educators seeking high-quality science education products. From telescopes that reveal the wonders of the cosmos to chemistry sets that unlock the mysteries of matter, we provide the tools that inspire the next generation of scientists, engineers, and innovators.

Our comprehensive catalog features carefully selected products across five major scientific disciplines. Every item in our collection has been tested by educators and meets the highest standards for safety, durability, and educational value.

Our Product Categories

Astronomy Products

Explore the universe with our wide range of telescopes, star charts, planetarium kits, and more. Perfect for classroom demonstrations and student projects.

Chemistry Products

Discover the world of chemistry with our laboratory equipment, chemical sets, and safety-tested materials. All kits include comprehensive lesson plans.

Physics Products
